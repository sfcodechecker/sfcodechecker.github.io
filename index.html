In 2018, the Thomson Reuters Foundation rated India as the most dangerous country in the world for women. Rates of domestic abuse are very high, and violence towards women is often seen as appropriate. Women and children who are victims can be left homeless and vulnerable.

Anglican Aid is partnering with the Shalom Global Foundation to support The Bangalore Women’s Safe House. The Safe House is assisting women in India seeking refuge from their partners by providing safety and hope.
